ClipHarbor Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

ClipHarbor ("we", "our") respects your privacy. Our application serves as a secure local clipboard manager designed to be offline-first.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your clipboard history or personal data to external servers. ClipHarbor operates completely offline on your macOS device.

2. Local Storage

All clipboard history and snippets are stored locally in your Application Support folder. You have full ownership and control over your data, including the ability to clear history at any time.

3. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at: contact@alanferreira.com
