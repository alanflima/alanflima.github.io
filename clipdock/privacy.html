Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

ClipDock ("we", "our") respects your privacy. Our application is designed to be offline-first and private by default.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations are offline and local. ClipDock operates completely on your macOS device without connecting to external servers.

2. Local Storage

All clipboard history, snippets, and preferences are stored locally in your Application Support folder. You have full ownership and control over your data, including the ability to clear your history at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

ClipDock does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@alanferreira.com
