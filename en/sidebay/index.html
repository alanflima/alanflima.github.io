SideBay

Your Lateral File Shelf

The invisible "External Drive" for your desktop. Keep your Desktop clean by dragging temporary files to the screen edge. A native side drawer that only appears when you need it.

Download on Mac App Store
Features

Your files, always at hand.

Fluid Drag & Drop

Drag files, images or text to the right edge of the screen to "park" them instantly. Drop on the shelf and retrieve with a click.

Natural Gestures

Open the drawer by simply hovering the mouse at the edge or with a light cursor "Shake". A tactile and responsive experience.

Native Design (Luminescence)

Translucent visual with Glassmorphism that integrates seamlessly with macOS Sonoma/Sequoia. Feels like it came pre-installed.
