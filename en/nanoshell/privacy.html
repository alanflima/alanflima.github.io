Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

NanoShell ("we", "our") respects your privacy. Our application is designed to be offline-first and private by default.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations are offline and local. NanoShell operates completely on your macOS device without connecting to external servers.

2. Local Storage

All code snippets, command history, and preferences are stored locally in your Application Support folder. You have full ownership and control over your data.

3. Third-Party Services

NanoShell does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@alanferreira.com
